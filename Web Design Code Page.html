Habitat

Living Conditions:

Food

Diet:

  • Whale Sharks eat small animals such as Jelly Fish, Squid, Plankton, and Krill.
  • Whale Sharks use 7.5 hours per day just to eat.
  • When eating, Whale Sharks gulp down ocean water and animals due to not using their teeth when feeding.
